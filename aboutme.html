About Me

My name is Romel Williams and I am a web and software developer from Bear, DE. I am a recent graduate from Wilmington University with a Masters Degree in Management and Management Information Systems.

I am an active person who enjoys playing sports, reading, listening to music and participating in community service.

My passion with code started back in high school when I started creating simple programs on my calculator to help me study. After talking to the professor to make sure it was ok he told me, "if you know the formulas well enough plug them into an application, you understand the lesson."

I am a member who has contributed several programs to ticalc.org; an online community where applications from math programs to games are available to download for TI calculators.

A quote from Anthony J. D'Angelo, "Never stop learning; knowledge doubles every fourteen months" speaks to me especially with advancing technologies. It is imperative to keep up to date with changes in software, philosophies and even the jargon.

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me.
